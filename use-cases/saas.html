Event-Driven Architecture & Product Analytics at Scale

We engineer the data backbone for high-growth SaaS platforms — from real-time product analytics and personalization engines to multi-tenant data architectures that scale to millions of users.

The Challenge

Scaling Data Infrastructure at the Pace of Product Growth

SaaS companies face a unique data paradox: product velocity demands real-time analytics, yet rapid growth creates exponential data complexity. Teams struggle with data silos across product, marketing, and customer success — while the underlying infrastructure groans under ever-increasing event volumes.

CircuitFern builds event-driven data architectures purpose-designed for SaaS scale — systems that ingest billions of events, power sub-second personalization, and deliver self-service analytics to every team without compromising multi-tenant data isolation.
